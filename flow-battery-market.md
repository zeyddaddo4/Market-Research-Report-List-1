<p><p>A flow battery is a type of electrochemical energy storage device that utilizes two electrolyte solutions, which are stored in external tanks, that flow through a cell stack to produce electricity. This technology enables scalable energy storage, making it ideal for renewable energy integration and grid stability. Flow batteries are particularly advantageous for applications requiring long-duration energy storage due to their ability to decouple energy and power.</p><p>The flow battery market is experiencing substantial growth, driven by the increasing demand for renewable energy sources and the need for reliable energy storage solutions. Factors contributing to this expansion include advancements in flow battery technology, decreasing costs of materials, and heightened focus on sustainability and reducing carbon footprints. </p><p>Emerging applications in electric vehicles and off-grid energy systems are further boosting market potential.
