<p><p>The railway valves market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Gate Valves** offer minimal flow resistance and are used for on/off control.</p><p>2. **Control Valves** regulate flow and pressure, ensuring safe operations. </p><p>3. **Ball Valves** provide quick shut-off and low friction loss, ideal for rapid flow regulation. </p><p>4. **Butterfly Valves** are compact and lightweight, suitable for large flow applications due to their quick operation. </p><p>5. **Others** encompass specialty valves designed for specific railway system needs. Each type plays a crucial role in railway safety and efficiency.</p></p>
