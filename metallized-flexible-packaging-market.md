<p><p>The Metallized Flexible Packaging market comprises primarily two types: Aluminium Foil-based Flexible Packaging and Metalized Film Flexible Packaging. Aluminium Foil-based options provide excellent barrier properties, ensuring product freshness and protection against moisture, light, and oxygen, making them ideal for food and pharmaceutical applications. Metalized Film Flexible Packaging, on the other hand, uses thin layers of metalized plastic films, offering similar protective qualities while being lighter and more flexible. Both types enhance product appeal and sustainability in various industries.</p></p>
