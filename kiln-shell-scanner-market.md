<p><p>The Kiln Shell Scanner market encompasses two primary types: Standard Scanners and Stereo Scanners. Standard Scanners utilize a single camera system to monitor the kiln shell's surface temperature and detect anomalies, providing essential data for operational efficiency. In contrast, Stereo Scanners employ a dual-camera setup for enhanced precision, capturing 3D representations of the kiln's geometry, which allows for more accurate assessments of structural integrity. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing kiln performance and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
