<p><strong>The Laminated Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure Laminate (HPL)</li><li>Continuous Pressure Laminate (CPL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminated panels market comprises High Pressure Laminate (HPL) and Continuous Pressure Laminate (CPL). HPL is created by applying heat and pressure to multiple layers of resin-impregnated paper, resulting in a durable and versatile surface ideal for countertops, furniture, and wall panels. CPL, on the other hand, involves a continuous process where resin-soaked materials pass through a series of rollers, producing thinner, flexible laminates commonly used in applications like furniture and interior design. Both types cater to different aesthetic and functional needs.</p></p>
