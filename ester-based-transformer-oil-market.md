<p><ul><li>Natural Ester Transformer Fluid</li><li>Synthetic Ester Transformer Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ester-based transformer oil market is primarily divided into two types: natural ester transformer fluid and synthetic ester transformer oil. Natural ester fluids are derived from vegetable oils, offering high biodegradability and fire resistance, making them environmentally friendly alternatives. Synthetic ester transformer oils, on the other hand, are chemically engineered, providing superior thermal properties and oxidative stability. Both types enhance the safety and efficiency of transformers, contributing to a growing preference for eco-friendly and high-performance insulating fluids in the energy sector.</p></p>
