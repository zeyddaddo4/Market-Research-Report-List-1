<p><p>The soil auger market is categorized into two primary types: hand-held and airborne. Hand-held augers are manually operated, typically used for small-scale applications like garden and landscaping projects, allowing users to extract soil samples easily. Both types serve essential roles in agriculture, construction, and environmental monitoring, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>
