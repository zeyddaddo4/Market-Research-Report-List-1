<p><p>Dissolved Gas Analyzers (DGAs) are instruments designed to monitor and analyze electrical insulating fluids in power transformers. They measure the concentration of specific gases, such as hydrogen, methane, ethane, and acetylene, which can indicate various types of degradation or faults within transformers. By detecting these gases, DGAs provide critical insights into the condition of electrical equipment, assisting in proactive maintenance and reducing the risk of failures.</p><p>The Dissolved Gas Analyzers Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for reliable electrical infrastructure, particularly in renewable energy and utility sectors. The growing emphasis on predictive maintenance to mitigate unplanned downtimes is also contributing to market expansion.
