PPEK Film (Polyetherketone) is recognized for its high-temperature properties. PVF Film (Polyvinyl Fluoride) features outstanding insulation and resistance to chemicals. Other films in the market may include specialized materials catering to niche applications, enhancing the versatility and performance of electrical insulation solutions.</p></p>
