<p><p>The solar-powered greenhouse fan market features two primary types: circular and exhaust fans. Circular fans, typically designed for even air distribution, are ideal for circulating warm air throughout the greenhouse, promoting consistent temperatures and humidity levels. Exhaust fans, on the other hand, are crucial for maintaining optimal growing conditions by expelling hot, stale air and drawing in cooler air from outside. Both types enhance plant growth efficiency while minimizing energy costs, leveraging renewable solar energy for sustainable greenhouse management.</p></p>
