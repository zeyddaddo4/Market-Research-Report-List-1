<p><p>Bisphenol F epoxy resins are categorized based on their viscosity, which affects their application and processing characteristics. Low viscosity resins are fluid and suitable for applications requiring good wetting and penetration, such as coatings and adhesives. Medium viscosity resins offer a balance, providing good flow and mechanical properties, often used in construction and automotive sectors. High viscosity resins, being thicker, are ideal for applications requiring enhanced structural integrity and resistance, commonly utilized in molding and casting processes.</p></p>
