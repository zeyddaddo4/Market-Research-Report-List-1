<p><p>The commercial solar panel market primarily features three types: monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film panels. Monocrystalline panels, made from single-crystal silicon, are known for high efficiency and space suitability, making them ideal for limited areas. Polycrystalline panels, composed of multiple silicon crystals, are generally less efficient but more affordable, appealing to budget-conscious buyers. Thin-film panels, made from various materials, are lightweight and flexible, suitable for diverse applications, albeit with lower efficiency. Each type meets specific commercial energy needs.</p></p>
