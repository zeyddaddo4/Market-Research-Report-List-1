<p><strong>The Handheld VOC Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld VOC meter market comprises various types, primarily including Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID meters utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, providing rapid detection of volatile organic compounds with high sensitivity. In contrast, MOS sensors rely on changes in electrical resistance to detect VOCs, offering a cost-effective solution for general applications. Both technologies are essential for real-time air quality monitoring, personal safety, and environmental assessments, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
