<p><p>The home battery storage system market primarily features two types of batteries: lead-acid and lithium-ion (Li-ion). In contrast, lithium-ion batteries offer higher energy density, longer life cycles, and faster charging capabilities, making them ideal for more extensive home energy storage needs. As renewable energy adoption grows, demand for lithium-ion solutions is increasing rapidly due to their efficiency.</p></p>
