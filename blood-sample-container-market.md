<p><p>The blood sample container market is primarily divided into two types: plastic and glass. Plastic containers are lightweight, shatterproof, and often cost-effective, making them popular in clinical settings. They may also feature various additives for specific tests. Glass containers, on the other hand, offer superior inertness and stability, reducing the risk of chemical interaction with the sample, which is crucial for certain assays. Both types cater to different laboratory needs, balancing durability, safety, and sample integrity.</p></p>
