<p><strong>The Latex Examination Gloves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Free</li><li>Powdered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Latex examination gloves are categorized into two main types: powdered and powder-free. Powdered gloves are treated with cornstarch to facilitate easy donning, reducing friction when putting them on. This type, however, may pose allergenic risks due to powder residue. Conversely, powder-free gloves are manufactured without any powder, minimizing the risk of allergic reactions and contamination. They often provide a more secure grip and are preferred in settings requiring high hygiene standards, such as medical and laboratory environments.</p></p>
