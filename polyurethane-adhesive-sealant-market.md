<p><strong>The Polyurethane Adhesive Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane adhesive sealant market is primarily divided into two main types: one-component and two-component. One-component sealants are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, two-component sealants require mixing a resin and a hardener before application, providing enhanced strength and durability. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors, each offering unique advantages based on the specific bonding or sealing needs of a project.</p></p>
