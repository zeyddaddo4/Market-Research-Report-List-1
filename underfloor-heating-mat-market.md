<p><p>The underfloor heating mat market is primarily divided into two types: electric and water-based systems. Electric underfloor heating mats use electric cables or heating elements embedded within mats to provide warmth, making installation quick and suitable for various spaces. In contrast, water-based systems circulate warm water through pipe networks beneath the floor, offering energy efficiency and consistent heating. Both types have their advantages, catering to different preferences and installation requirements, thus contributing to the growing popularity of underfloor heating solutions.</p></p>
