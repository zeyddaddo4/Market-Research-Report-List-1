<p><ul><li>Mono-Split</li><li>Multi-Split</li><li>VRF</li><li>Floor Ceiling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Split air conditioning systems are categorized into several types: Mono-Split, Multi-Split, VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow), and Floor-Ceiling systems. Mono-Split systems consist of one indoor and one outdoor unit, perfect for individual rooms. Multi-Split systems allow multiple indoor units to connect to a single outdoor unit, ideal for larger spaces. VRF systems provide precise temperature control with varying refrigerant flow, suitable for commercial applications. Floor-Ceiling units are versatile, installed to offer efficient heating and cooling from either position, suitable for diverse environments.</p></p>
