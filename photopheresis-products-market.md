<p><p>The photopheresis products market is categorized into open and closed systems. Open systems involve the collection of blood from donors, treating it with photoactive agents, and returning it to the patient, allowing for greater flexibility in processing. Closed systems, on the other hand, maintain a sterile environment throughout the treatment, minimizing contamination risks and enhancing safety. Both systems are integral in photopheresis therapy, catering to different clinical needs and operational preferences within healthcare settings.</p></p>
