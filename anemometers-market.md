<p><ul><li>Cup Anemometers</li><li>Vane Anemometers</li><li>Hot-wire Anemometers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anemometer market comprises various types catering to different wind measurement needs. Cup anemometers consist of spinning cups that measure wind speed, making them popular in meteorology and wind energy applications. Vane anemometers, equipped with a rotating blade, measure airflow in ducts and can assess both speed and direction. Hot-wire anemometers utilize a heated wire to detect changes in air velocity through cooling effects. Additionally, the market includes other specialized types suited for unique environmental conditions or technical requirements.</p></p>
