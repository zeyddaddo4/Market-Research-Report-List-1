<p><p>Pharmacy blister packaging involves various materials, each offering specific benefits. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is cost-effective and versatile, while PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) provides enhanced barrier protection against moisture and oxygen. Polyamide (PA) offers superior strength and thermal resistance, making it suitable for higher temperature applications. Polyethylene (PE) is flexible and offers good moisture resistance. Aluminum is widely used for its excellent barrier properties, ensuring product integrity. Other materials may include combinations or alternatives suited for specific pharmaceutical needs, catering to different packaging requirements.</p></p>
