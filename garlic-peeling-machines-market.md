Wet-type machines utilize water to soften the skins for easier peeling, enhancing yield and reducing waste. In contrast, dry-type machines rely on mechanical mechanisms and air pressure for peeling, offering a more energy-efficient solution without the need for water. Both types cater to different processing demands, helping businesses optimize their garlic processing operations while maintaining quality and efficiency.</p></p>
