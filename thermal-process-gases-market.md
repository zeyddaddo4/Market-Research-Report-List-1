<p><p>The thermal process gases market is categorized into heat-generating gases and protective gases. Heat-generating gases, such as natural gas and propane, are utilized for combustion in industrial heating applications, enabling processes like metalworking and material processing. Protective gases, including argon and nitrogen, are employed to create inert environments that prevent oxidation and contamination during welding and other high-temperature processes. Both types are essential for enhancing efficiency, safety, and quality in various thermal applications across industries.</p></p>
