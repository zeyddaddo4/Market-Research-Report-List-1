<p><p>The CNC shot peening machine market comprises various shot types, each serving distinct purposes. Cut wire shot is made from steel wire, known for its hardness and uniformity, ideal for enhancing surface fatigue resistance. Glass bead shot offers a non-destructive method for cleaning and finishing surfaces, while steel shot is favored for its durability in heavy-duty applications. Ceramic bead shot is lightweight and prevents surface distortion, making it suitable for delicate components. Each type optimizes the peening process for specific material properties and surface finishes.</p></p>
