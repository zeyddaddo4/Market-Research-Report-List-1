<p><strong>The Welding Helmets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Welding Helmet</li><li>Auto Darkening Welding Helmets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding helmets market comprises two primary types: passive welding helmets and auto-darkening welding helmets. Passive welding helmets feature fixed dark lenses that provide consistent eye protection but require manual adjustment for different welding tasks. In contrast, auto-darkening welding helmets are equipped with sensors that automatically darken the lens when exposed to welding light, enabling welders to switch between tasks without stopping. This adaptability enhances comfort and safety, making auto-darkening helmets increasingly popular among professionals in welding industries.</p></p>
