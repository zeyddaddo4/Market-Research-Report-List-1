<p><strong>The Implant Surgery Retractor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Fixed Traction Device</li><li>Internal Fixed Traction Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implant surgery retractor market is divided into two main types: external fixed traction devices and internal fixed traction devices. External fixed traction devices are used outside the body to stabilize and maintain the position of bones or tissues during surgery. They offer ease of access for surgical intervention. In contrast, internal fixed traction devices are implanted within the body, providing support and stabilization for fractured bones or damaged tissues, promoting healing while remaining unobtrusive. Both types enhance surgical outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
