<p><p>The mammography X-ray unit market is primarily categorized into analog and digital systems. In contrast, digital mammography employs advanced technology to create electronic images, offering enhanced image quality, faster processing times, and the ability to manipulate images for better diagnostics. Digital systems are increasingly preferred due to their efficiency, ease of use, and reduced radiation exposure, driving their market growth.</p></p>
