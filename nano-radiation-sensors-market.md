<p><strong>The Nano Radiation Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scintillation Detectors</li><li>Solid-State Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nano radiation sensors encompass advanced technologies for detecting radiation at exceptionally small scales. Scintillation detectors use materials that emit light in response to ionizing radiation, converting this light into electrical signals for precise measurement. Solid-state detectors, on the other hand, utilize semiconductor materials to directly translate radiation interactions into electronic signals, offering high resolution and efficiency. Both types are critical in applications like healthcare, environmental monitoring, and nuclear safety, driving growth in the nano radiation sensors market.</p></p>
