<p><strong>The Semi-rigid Airship Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Helium Airship</li><li>Gas Airship</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-rigid airship market is primarily categorized into two types: helium airships and gas airships. Helium airships utilize helium as a lifting gas, offering safety and environmental advantages due to helium's non-flammable nature. Gas airships, on the other hand, typically employ lighter-than-air gases like hydrogen, which provide higher lift but come with increased risks due to flammability. Both types serve various applications, including tourism, advertising, and surveillance, driven by their unique characteristics and operational benefits.</p></p>
