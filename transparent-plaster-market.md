<p><ul><li>Non Perforated Transparent Plaster</li><li>Perforated Transparent Plaster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transparent plaster market comprises two primary types: non-perforated and perforated transparent plaster. Non-perforated transparent plaster features a solid, seamless finish, ideal for aesthetic applications where visual continuity is desired. It provides a smooth surface that enhances the appearance of interiors. In contrast, perforated transparent plaster contains small holes or openings, allowing for improved breathability and moisture management. This type is often used in environments requiring ventilation, making it suitable for areas like bathrooms and kitchens while maintaining a stylish look.</p></p>
