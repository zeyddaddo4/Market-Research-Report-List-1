<p><p>Nucleic acid extraction and purification reagents are essential for isolating DNA and RNA from various biological samples. The Magnetic Bead Method utilizes magnetic beads to selectively bind nucleic acids, allowing for efficient separation using a magnetic field. The Centrifugal Column Method involves passing samples through columns containing silica membranes, which capture nucleic acids due to their affinity for the silica under specific conditions. Other methods may include enzyme-based approaches or precipitation techniques, catering to diverse sample types and research needs.</p></p>
