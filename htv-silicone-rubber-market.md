<p><p>The HTV (High-Temperature Vulcanized) silicone rubber market encompasses various processing methods, including compression molding, extrusion, and injection molding. Compression molding involves shaping rubber by applying heat and pressure, making it ideal for large parts. Extrusion creates continuous shapes by forcing silicone through a die, suitable for seals and gaskets. Injection molding allows for intricate designs and higher production efficiency by injecting silicone into molds. Other methods may include blow molding and calendaring, catering to diverse applications in automotive, industrial, and consumer goods sectors.</p></p>
