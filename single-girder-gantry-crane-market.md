<p><p>The single girder gantry crane market can be divided primarily into two types: gantry cranes and half-gantry cranes. Gantry cranes feature a full framework that supports the hoist and allows for lateral movement, making them ideal for outdoor applications with heavy loads. Half-gantry cranes, on the other hand, have a partial support structure, enabling them to operate in tighter spaces while still providing significant lifting capabilities. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing efficiency in material handling and construction tasks.</p></p>
