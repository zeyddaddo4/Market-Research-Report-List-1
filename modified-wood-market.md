<p><strong>The Modified Wood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermally Modified Wood</li><li>Chemical Modified Wood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modified wood market encompasses two primary types: thermally modified wood and chemically modified wood. Thermally modified wood undergoes heat treatment to enhance durability, stability, and resistance to decay, elevating its performance for outdoor and high-moisture applications. In contrast, chemically modified wood incorporates various chemicals to improve properties like water resistance, insect repellence, and dimensional stability. Both modifications aim to extend the lifespan and applicability of wood products, catering to eco-conscious consumers and industries seeking sustainable materials.</p></p>
